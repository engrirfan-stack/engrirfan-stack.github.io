Research

My research develops scalable optimization and simulation methods for resilient electric power distribution systems. The central question is how to restore the maximum feasible load after an outage while satisfying physics, topology, DER, storage, and uncertainty constraints.

Black-Start Restoration

I develop sequential black-start restoration models for active distribution systems. These models coordinate controllable switches, distributed energy resources, grid-forming resources, and load pickup decisions while enforcing voltage, thermal, radiality, and resource constraints.

  • Mixed-integer restoration formulations for distribution feeders and microgrids.
  • Grid-forming inverter and DER coordination constraints.
  • Cold load pickup and time-dependent load behavior.
  • Restoration sequence feasibility and operational constraint validation.

Two-Stage Robust Maximal Load Delivery

I study robust maximal load delivery for large-scale distribution systems under uncertain load conditions. The framework separates restoration and dispatch decisions from worst-case feasibility evaluation under uncertain demand.

  • Master-subproblem decomposition for restoration planning.
  • Uncertainty sets for active and reactive load variation.
  • Worst-case feasibility checks and feasibility-cut generation.
  • Large-scale studies on modified Iowa 240-node systems with PV, BESS, diesel generation, and switchable sections.

DER, Storage, and Microgrid Coordination

My work studies DER-supported restoration using photovoltaic generation, battery energy storage systems, diesel generators, and inverter-based resources.

  • Battery charging/discharging and state-of-charge constrained dispatch.
  • PV availability and renewable variability under adverse weather conditions.
  • Microgrid networking for critical-load restoration.
  • Grid-forming and grid-following inverter operation in restoration studies.

Robust Operation of Data-Center Power Infrastructure

This is a developing research direction. I am extending my restoration, robust optimization, and DER-coordination background toward data-center electric infrastructure. The focus is resilient operation of high-density electric loads under uncertainty, contingency, backup generation limits, storage constraints, renewable variability, and utility-grid operating limits.

Simulation and Validation Workflows

I build computational workflows that connect OpenDSS feeder models, Julia/JuMP optimization, PowerModelsDistribution.jl, PowerModelsONM.jl, Python automation, and solver-based analysis.
