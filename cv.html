Professional Summary

Researcher in power and energy systems focused on grid resilience and distribution system restoration. Develops optimization-based methods for black-start restoration, maximal load delivery, and uncertainty-aware operation of large-scale distribution networks with inverter-based resources and microgrids. Builds scalable computational workflows using PowerModelsONM, OpenDSS, and mixed-integer optimization to support restoration decision-making under extreme events and variable load conditions.
